Joe Freimuth is a music and education entrepreneur. Joe is the co-founder of AV Music Group, which creates software for musicians. He is also the co-founder of NerdCoach, which creates video-game inspired mobile study apps that make standardized test prep fun and portable. Previously, he founded and managed Astor Studios, a music recording and production studio that worked with artists such as Andy Caldwell, Basskleph, Jonas Tempel, Zak Waters, Hot Mouth, Just Off Turner and Brave Native. His songs have been featured on network television and major motion pictures. Joe graduated from Duke University and currently lives in Los Angeles, CA.
